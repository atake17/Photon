We are Photon

Our goal: to make the use of Bitcoin more accessible to both consumers and retailers.

    Consumers

  • No Fees

    You only pay us what you spend. Nothing more.

  • Unlimited Spending

    With our system, you will never have to predict how much you will spend.

  • Complete Anonymity

    Retailers will never get your information, keeping your identity secure.

    Retailers

  • NFC Technology

    With near-field communication systems, transactions occur instantly.

  • Pay Less

    While most credit cards charge 1-3% per transaction, we only ask for .5%

  • BTC Accessibility

    More accessibility to the BTC community results in more customers.

Why Photon?

Photon enables you to use digital currency without even having to think about it.

Photon is the only way to use Bitcoin with a credit card system. Currently, you have to convert dollars to Bitcoin, which are deposited in your Bitcoin wallet, every time you want to use Bitcoin. This forces you to predict how much you are going to spend and to actually wait for the conversion to occur, which can take around half-an-hour. Bitcoin debit cards exist, but they limit your spending capabilities and force you to deal with these issues every time you want to reload your card. With Photon, there’s no need to think about how you pay, so you can focus on what’s important to you.

The Process

  • Customers register with Photon and get the app, free-of-charge.

  • Customers use their Photon app to buy products and other stuff.

  • Retailers get paid by Photon, with a small fee being deducted for each transaction.

  • Customers pay Photon what they spent at the end of the month. No fees or headaches.
